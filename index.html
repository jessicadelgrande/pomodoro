This is a simple timer to help you manage your time. It's based on the Pomodoro technique.

When you're ready to begin your task, press The Pomodoro button. You will have 25 minutes of uninterrupted time until the Pomodoro is done.

Take a 5 minute break. Press The Short Break button.

After 4 Pomodoros, press The Long Break button and take 30 minutes to step away from your task.
